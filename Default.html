School Air

Empowering schools through environmental data

Next

Why?

Air quality stations, in our selected pilot city, Barcelona, are not providing an accurate enough real time status for schools in their immediate area. Existing sensors are primarily in green public parks and on high spots of the city such as radio towers at the top of hills. It was determined that there is an urgent need for schools to be in touch with the air quality of their particular locale â€“ so that they may take protective actions as necessary.

To protect the health of our young students and provide an opportunity for a meaningful technical project, Alella Green Tech is sponsoring schoolAIR: students will use the latest microcontrollers and various different types of sensors, to build an air quality and weather station from scratch. This will give students an excellent foundation in environmental science, data science, and programming. Air & Weather stations will be installed at each participating school.
